# LPRI – Research Internship

**LPRI** is the research laboratory of my school **EMSI (École Marocaine des Sciences de l’Ingénieur)**.

LPRI stands for:

**Laboratoire Pluridisciplinaire de Recherche et Innovation**

Since my long-term goal is to pursue a **PhD after completing my Master's degree**, I decided to gain early exposure to research during my undergraduate studies. My idea was to start learning how research labs work, develop technical skills, and build experience that would help me become a stronger candidate for future PhD programs.

For that reason, I reached out to the LPRI laboratory and expressed my motivation to learn, improve my skills, and contribute to research projects.

I was accepted as an **intern**, and **Soufina Ameur** was assigned as my mentor.

The internship will start with **IoT-related projects**, and later may expand into other areas such as robotics and intelligent systems.

This folder documents my work, notes, and progress during my internship at LPRI.

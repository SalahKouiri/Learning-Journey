# LPRI Internship

This folder documents my internship at **LPRI (Laboratoire Pluridisciplinaire de Recherche et Innovation)** at **EMSI**.

The goal of this internship is to gain early experience in **research and engineering projects**, especially in areas related to **robotics, IoT, and intelligent systems**.

During this internship, I am working under the supervision of **Soufina Ameur**, who is guiding me through the learning process and helping me develop the skills needed to work on research-oriented projects.

## Objectives

The main objectives of this internship are:

- Learn how research projects are structured and conducted
- Develop practical engineering skills
- Work with technologies related to **IoT, robotics, and intelligent systems**
- Build a strong technical and research foundation for future **Master’s and PhD studies**

## Current Phase

At the beginning of the internship, I am focusing on learning the fundamentals and building small projects to understand the technologies involved.

This includes topics such as:

- IoT systems and sensor integration
- Communication protocols (MQTT, etc.)
- Embedded systems
- Robotics platforms and tools

## Documentation

This folder will contain:

- Notes about what I learn during the internship
- Small experiments and tests
- Project ideas and implementations
- Progress logs and reflections

The goal is to document the learning process and keep track of the skills and knowledge gained throughout the internship.
